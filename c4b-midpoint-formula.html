Section 1 — Why This Matters

Finding the Center

You now know how to find the distance between two points. But sometimes the question isn't how far apart two points are — it's where is the exact middle? That's what the Midpoint Formula gives you.

🔵 Why you need this for circles

If you're given the two endpoints of a diameter of a circle, the center is the midpoint of that diameter. Without the midpoint formula, you can't write the equation of the circle. That connection is coming up next.

Section 2 — The Formula

Average the Coordinates

To find the midpoint of a segment, find the average of the x-coordinates and the average of the y-coordinates of the two endpoints.
