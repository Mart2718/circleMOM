Section 1 — What Is a Circle, Really?

The Distance Formula in Disguise

A circle is the set of all points in a plane that are a fixed distance from a given center point.

That fixed distance is the radius . The center is the point . Any point that lies on the circle is exactly units away from .

That last sentence is the distance formula: . Square both sides and you have the equation of a circle.

Any point (x, y) on the circle satisfies the distance formula with the center (h, k) and radius r.
